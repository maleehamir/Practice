About Deep Sea Exploration

The deep sea is one of the last frontiers on Earth, covering more than 60% of our planet's surface. It is home to a vast array of unique organisms and geological features.

Why Explore the Deep Sea?

  • Discover new species and ecosystems.
  • Understand the effects of climate change.
  • Explore underwater geology and resources.
